About Our Innovation

🎯 Our Vision

To make housing affordable, accessible and stress-free for Nigerians by connecting landlords directly to tenants.

🚫 Problem We Solve

Many citizens lose money through excessive agent fees, fake listings and unnecessary middlemen exploitation.

💡 Our Solution

DirectRent Nigeria provides a trusted digital platform where landlords, tenants and developers connect directly.

🏗 Beyond Rentals

We also connect building project developers directly to customers and investors.

📱 Why It Matters

This app-like platform reduces house-hunting stress, saves time and lowers housing costs.

🇳🇬 National Impact

Supports economic growth, digital inclusion, housing transparency and youth-led innovation in Nigeria.

Founder Statement

DirectRent Nigeria was created from a simple idea: every Nigerian deserves fair access to housing without exploitation. By using technology, we can transform how people rent homes and buy projects across the country.

Our National Impact Vision

🏠 10,000+ Homes

Targeting easier access to affordable housing nationwide.

👥 50,000+ Users

Connecting tenants, landlords, buyers and developers.

💸 Reduced Exploitation

Helping citizens avoid excessive agent charges.

📱 Digital Inclusion

Making property access simple for all generations.

Founder Story

DirectRent Nigeria was inspired by the everyday struggles many Nigerians face while searching for homes. From fake listings to exploitative charges and stressful processes, housing access has become harder than it should be. This platform was built to restore trust, reduce costs, and connect people directly through technology.
